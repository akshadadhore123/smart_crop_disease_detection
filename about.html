About AgroVision AI

AgroVision AI is a state-of-the-art AI system designed to detect plant diseases from crop leaf images using Deep Learning. It provides precise disease prediction and smart treatment recommendations.

🌍 Our Vision

To revolutionize agriculture with AI innovation by providing farmers fast, affordable, and accurate crop disease diagnosis.

🎯 Our Mission

To reduce crop loss, increase productivity, and empower farmers with smart digital tools powered by AI & Machine Learning.

⚙ Technology Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Backend: Node.js / Express
AI Model: Deep Learning (CNN)
Dataset: Crop Leaf Image Dataset
